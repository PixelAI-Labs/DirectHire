# Motion Philosophy

Motion should:

- Explain
- Guide
- Reinforce hierarchy

Motion should never:

- Distract
- Entertain for no reason

---

# Motion Levels

## Level 1

Micro interactions

100–200ms

Examples:

- Hover
- Focus
- Click

---

## Level 2

Interface motion

300–600ms

Examples:

- Cards
- Drawers
- Modals

---

## Level 3

Narrative motion

800–1500ms

Examples:

- Agent communication
- Workflow diagrams
- Hero animations
