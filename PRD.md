# DirectHire
# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0

## Executive Summary

DirectHire is an autonomous multi-agent recruitment ecosystem where AI-powered Career Agents and Hiring Agents collaborate to streamline hiring from application to offer.

The platform helps candidates discover and optimize opportunities while helping recruiters identify, evaluate, and hire top talent through intelligent automation.

---

## Vision

Every candidate has a Career Agent.

Every recruiter has a Hiring Agent.

These agents collaborate to optimize hiring while maintaining complete transparency and human oversight.

---

## User Roles

### Candidate

- Career Agent
